Frequently Asked Questions :

What is a booking reference?

A booking reference, also known as a Passenger Name Record (PNR), is a unique identifier for your reservation. It typically consists of six alphanumeric characters (e.g., B8XYZ6) or a 12-digit numerical characters.

What is the validity of an e-ticket?

The validity of an e-ticket depends on the fare rules associated with your booking. Typically, tickets are valid for one year from the date of issue.

How do I select my seat?

Seat selection is subject to availability and the fare you have purchased. You can select your seat during the booking process or through your online account after booking.
